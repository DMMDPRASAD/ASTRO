Planets and houses are two very important components in astrology-in fact basic block or foundation of entire astrology. In this page a brief introduction will be given about planets - role &characteristics

PLANETS

In astrology there are 9 planets that are constantly in motion. Each planet has it's own evolution mythological stories, characteristics and influence different parts of life.

The nine planets are:

  1. SUN
  2. MOON
  3. MARS
  4. MERCURY
  5. JUPITER
  6. VENUS
  7. SATURN
  8. RAHU
  9. KETU

NOTE:

Rahu and Ketu do not have physical existence but they are mathematical point found at the intersection of orbits of Sun and MOON


Now let's go in detail about each planet

Sun

Sun is the center of our solar system. He is the one who is holding all the planets through his gravitaitonal pull. All other planets revolve around him. So Sun is the major planet who has got influence over soul of human being. He is symbolised for purity. He has got a father character. He represents authority, power, government, government servants etc., The background color belongs to him

Moon

Moon is the second planet having influence over human mind. He also rule water bodies-oceans, seas etc.,

MARS
